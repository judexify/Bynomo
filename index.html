✦ How It Works

No complex mechanics. No hidden steps. Just connect, deposit, and trade.

01

Connect Your Wallet

Land on Bynomo and connect via MetaMask or Privy. Switch to BNB Mainnet and deposit BNB into your Bynomo trading wallet.

02

Select Amount & Mode

Click Bet, choose your investment amount, then pick Classic Mode (Up/Down with expiry) or Box Mode (tap multiplier tiles).

03

Place Your Trade

In Classic Mode, call Higher or Lower with a timeframe from 5s to 1m. In Box Mode, tap a multiplier tile — if the price line touches it, you win.

04

Oracle Settles Instantly

Pyth Network pulls the price at expiry in under 0.001ms. Win or lose, your house balance updates immediately. No on-chain tx required per bet.

05

Withdraw Anytime

Request a withdrawal and BNB is sent directly from the treasury wallet to yours. Profit minus loss settled transparently, on-chain.
